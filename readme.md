The Professional Bulk Drive Manager - Take absolute control over your portable storage. USB Master is a high-performance utility designed for professionals who handle multiple USB flash drives and External Hard Drives (HDD/SSD) daily.

Whether you are a photographer backing up shoots, an IT professional deploying data, or a creator managing archives, USB Master streamlines your workflow with one-click bulk operations.

### 🚀 BATCH OPERATIONS REDEFINED
Stop wasting time managing drives one by one. Our powerful engine allows you to:
• Bulk Eject: Safely remove all connected drives in a single click. No more "Disk not ejected properly" warnings.
• High-Speed Batch Copy: Distribute data from your Mac to multiple USBs simultaneously, or collect data from various drives back to your Mac.
• Rapid Clear: Empty multiple devices instantly without the need for manual file selection.

### 📂 UNIVERSAL DRIVE COMPATIBILITY
Built specifically for the macOS file system, supporting:
• USB-C & USB-A Flash Drives.
• External HDDs and SSDs.
• Removable SD Cards and portable media.

### 🛡️ PRIVACY & SECURITY BY DESIGN
• 100% Offline: Your data and file structures never leave your Mac.
• Safety First: Clear visual indicators for every drive (Capacity, Status, Serial Number) to ensure you always target the right device.
• System Integration: Works seamlessly with macOS Monterey, Ventura, and Sonoma.
